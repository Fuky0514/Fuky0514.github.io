Official: Starch Sausage Report Fake
Image source: Reveal News / Stock

近日，一份标题为《市面多数美式热狗成分造假，某州消费者协会发布警示》的“调查报告”在多个社交平台被大量转发。该文件仿冒官方文件格式与印章，并配以来源不明的“实验室数据”，宣称通过“大规模抽检”发现美式热狗产品普遍存在违规使用添加剂、以次充好等问题，引发部分消费者担忧并对相关产品的市场销售造成了直接影响。

Recently, a "research report" titled "Most American Hot Dogs on the Market Have False Ingredients; A Consumer Association in a Certain State Issues a Warning" has been widely shared on multiple social media platforms. This document imitates the format and seal of official documents and is accompanied by "laboratory data" of unknown origin, claiming that through "large-scale random inspections", it was found that American hot dog products generally have problems such as the illegal use of additives and passing off inferior goods as good ones, which has caused concern among some consumers and directly affected the market sales of related products.

随着事件影响扩大，被冒名提及的消费者协会及其上级主管机构迅速作出反应，发布了正式《声明通告》。《通告》明确指出，该所谓的“调查报告”纯属捏造，其提及的所谓“抽检”、“数据”和“结论”均无任何事实依据。文件中所使用的机构名称、公章样式及文号均系伪造。相关部门强调，从未授权或发布过任何关于“美式热狗行业普遍造假”的调查报告或风险警示。

As the impact of the incident expanded, the consumer association mentioned under a false name and its superior administrative body promptly responded and issued an official "Statement and Notice". The "Notice" clearly stated that the so-called "investigation report" was purely fabricated, and the so-called "random inspection", "data" and "conclusions" mentioned in it had no factual basis at all. The names of the institutions, the style of the official seals and the document numbers used in the document were all forged. Relevant departments emphasized that they had never authorized or released any investigation report or risk warning about "widespread fraud in the American hot dog industry".

初步网络溯源信息显示，这份伪造报告最早出现在一个以发布博眼球内容为主的个人自媒体账号上，随后被多个营销号转发。市场监管部门表示，正在依法对伪造公文、散布谣言的源头进行追查。

Preliminary online traceability information shows that this forged report first appeared on a personal self-media account that mainly publishes eye-catching content and was then forwarded by multiple marketing accounts. Market supervision departments stated that they are legally investigating the source of the forged official documents and the spread of rumors.

食品安全领域的专家指出，此类精准冒充官方、针对大众化食品的谣言危害性极大。它不仅直接侵害正规生产经营企业的合法权益，导致其无端遭受商誉损失，还不合理地引发公众对特定食品品类的安全恐慌，扰乱了正常的市场秩序和消费信心，并严重透支了官方权威发布渠道的公信力。

Experts in the field of food safety point out that such precise counterfeits of official information, targeting mass-market foods, are extremely harmful. They not only directly infringe upon the legitimate rights and interests of regular production and operation enterprises, causing them to suffer unjustified damage to their business reputation, but also unreasonably trigger public panic over the safety of specific food categories, disrupt the normal market order and consumer confidence, and seriously deplete the credibility of official authoritative release channels.

监管部门提醒广大消费者，获取食品安全信息应认准各级市场监督管理部门、卫生健康部门及消费者协会的官方网站、政务公众号等权威渠道。对于网络流传的各类“内部报告”，应注意核对发文单位的真实性，并可主动通过官方渠道进行核实，做到不轻信、不盲从、不传播。

Regulatory authorities remind consumers to obtain food safety information from official websites and government WeChat accounts of market supervision and administration departments, health and wellness departments, and consumer associations at all levels. For various "internal reports" circulating on the Internet, consumers should verify the authenticity of the issuing units and can proactively check through official channels to avoid gullibility, blind following, and spreading.

目前，相关网络平台已开始对已发现的谣言链接进行排查与处理。此次事件再次表明，在信息时代，甄别信息真伪是保护自身权益、维护市场环境的重要能力。对于恶意编造和传播虚假食品安全信息的行为，法律必将予以严惩。

Currently, relevant online platforms have begun to investigate and handle the discovered rumor links. This incident once again indicates that in the information age, the ability to distinguish the authenticity of information is an important capability for protecting one's own rights and interests and maintaining a healthy market environment. For maliciously fabricating and spreading false food safety information, the law will surely impose severe punishment.
