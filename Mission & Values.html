Mission and Values

Nowadays, with the continuous development of mobile phone technology, more and more people are spreading false information online. These false information have caused very serious negative impacts on the individuals involved. However, only a very small number of those who create and spread such information will be legally pursued and punished.

This work is presented in the form of an interactive website, with a news website as the main body, combined with video editing, story design and interactive animations, to expose false news and criticize it, and to call on people to be responsible for their words in the online environment.
