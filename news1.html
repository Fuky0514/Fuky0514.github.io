City's Silent Guardian Strikes Again, Identity Remains a Mystery
Brief: Mutant "Butcher" neutralized by mysterious Plantwoman before military could intervene.

Last night, the city's East District Third Dock once again became a breeding ground for genetically contaminated organisms. A mutant codenamed "Butcher" breached the quarantine zone, its secreted strong acid mucus and rapidly proliferating tumors causing severe damage to surrounding infrastructure. Yet, as in the three previous incidents, before military special forces could mount an effective strike, the mysterious entity citizens call Plantwoman descended once more, controlling the situation with her signature botanical powers.

Witnesses reported that just as the "Butcher" mutant was about to use its tentacles to destroy an oil storage tank, the ground suddenly fractured violently. Countless enhanced vines glowing with a faint blue light erupted, shackling the monster like living chains within seconds. Immediately after, from the shadows of the container yard, sharp, metal-reinforced bamboo spears rained down, precisely piercing the pulsating energy sacs across the creature's body, neutralizing its offensive capabilities.

The entire confrontation was eerily silent—no shouts, no gunpowder smoke, only the faint sounds of growing plants and the crushing of the monster's exoskeleton. As a final network of luminescent moss covered the creature's wounds, halting its regeneration, the mysterious green figure dissipated into the depths of the container maze like mist.

"We set a new record for our response time," Field Commander Li Zhen stated in a post-operation briefing. "But unfortunately, we failed to capture any trace of her presence this time, either. She appears and vanishes as unpredictably as the plants she summons."

As of now, the biological remains of the "Butcher" mutant have been fully recovered. Environmental agencies are assessing the contamination level from the incident. The identity, motives, and origin of the green guardian who repeatedly saves the city from peril, yet consistently refuses communication, remain an unsolved mystery.
