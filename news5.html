AI Refund Scam Reversed: Police Confirm 'Female' Customer Was Male
Image source: Reveal News / Stock

近日，一桩因“仅退款”纠纷引发的网络风波在警方介入后迎来反转。一名用户以食品变质为由，通过电商平台的“仅退款”功能向商家索赔，并在被要求举证时，提交了一段显示商品存在问题的视频。然而，商家在审核视频时发现了多处不合理之处——视频中物体的移动轨迹生硬，光影效果也存在明显的拼接痕迹。基于对AI造假手段的警惕，商家质疑视频真实性并拒绝了退款请求。

Recently, a online controversy caused by a dispute over "only refund" has undergone a reversal after the intervention of the police. A user, citing the spoilage of the food, claimed compensation from the merchant through the "only refund" function of the e-commerce platform. When asked to provide evidence, the user submitted a video showing the problem with the product. However, when the merchant reviewed the video, they discovered several unreasonable aspects - the movement trajectory of the objects in the video was stiff, and there were obvious signs of splicing in the lighting effect. Due to their vigilance against AI manipulation techniques, the merchant questioned the authenticity of the video and refused the refund request.

退款请求被拒后，事件并未平息。该用户以女性身份在多个社交平台持续发声，指控商家不仅售卖问题商品，还在纠纷中恶意泄露了其个人隐私信息，并附上了部分疑似被骚扰的短信截图。这些指控迅速点燃了网友的同情与愤怒，大量网民涌入商家的社交账号，对其发布的每一篇帖文进行举报，并通过私信对商家及其员工进行辱骂与人身威胁，导致商家正常的网络经营活动一度瘫痪。

After the refund request was rejected, the situation did not calm down. The user, identified as a woman, continued to voice her concerns on multiple social platforms, accusing the merchant of not only selling defective products but also maliciously disclosing her personal privacy information during the dispute, and attaching some screenshots of suspected harassing messages. These accusations quickly aroused the sympathy and anger of netizens, and a large number of netizens flooded the merchant's social media accounts, reporting every post they saw and abusing and threatening the merchant and its employees through private messages, causing the merchant's normal online business activities to be temporarily paralyzed.

为自证清白并维护权益，不堪其扰的商家最终选择报警处理。

In order to clear their own names and protect their rights, the harassed merchants eventually chose to report the matter to the police.

警方接到报案后，迅速对双方陈述及证据展开了详细调查。经核实，警方首先确认商家在纠纷处理过程中，仅通过平台内置聊天工具进行沟通，并未获取或传播该用户的任何隐私信息，所谓“泄露隐私”的指控并无事实依据。

After receiving the report, the police promptly conducted a detailed investigation into the statements and evidence from both parties. After verification, the police first confirmed that during the dispute resolution process, the merchant only communicated through the built-in chat tool of the platform and did not obtain or disseminate any private information of the user. The accusation of "leaking privacy" had no factual basis.

更令人意外的是，进一步的调查揭穿了该用户的另一重伪装。警方通过核实实名信息发现，在整个事件中以“女士”自称、并以此身份博取舆论同心的用户，实际身份是一名男性。同时，警方委托技术部门对起争议的“变质食品视频”进行了鉴定，报告证实该视频确系利用人工智能技术伪造生成。

What was even more surprising was that further investigation revealed another disguise of this user. The police, by verifying the real-name information, discovered that the user who claimed to be a "lady" throughout the incident and used this identity to gain public support actually was a man. At the same time, the police entrusted the technical department to examine the controversial "spoiled food video". The report confirmed that this video was indeed fabricated using artificial intelligence technology.

至此，事件真相大白。该名男性用户的行为已涉嫌利用伪造证据谋取不当利益，并在事后捏造事实、误导公众对商家进行网络攻击。目前，案件仍在进一步处理中，当事人可能需为其行为承担相应的法律后果。

At this point, the truth of the incident has come to light. The actions of this male user have been suspected of using forged evidence to seek improper benefits, and later fabricating facts to mislead the public and launch cyber attacks against the business. Currently, the case is still under further investigation, and the party involved may need to bear the corresponding legal consequences for their actions.

这起事件也引发了电商行业的广泛讨论。随着AI技术门槛降低，利用AI伪造图片、视频进行恶意索赔的案例时有发生，让不少商家蒙受损失。法律专家指出，此类行为已超出普通消费纠纷范畴，一旦查实，行为人可能面临行政拘留乃至诈骗罪的指控。业内人士呼吁，平台应建立更严格的证据审核机制，并利用技术手段识别AI伪造内容，以维护健康的网络交易环境

This incident has also sparked extensive discussions within the e-commerce industry. With the lowering of the threshold for AI technology, cases of using AI to forge images and videos for malicious claims occur frequently, causing losses to many merchants. Legal experts point out that such behavior has gone beyond the scope of ordinary consumer disputes. Once verified, the perpetrators may face administrative detention or even charges of fraud. Industry insiders call for platforms to establish stricter evidence review mechanisms and utilize technological means to identify AI-generated content, in order to maintain a healthy online trading environment.
